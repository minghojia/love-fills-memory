# Brand Belief System｜品牌信念系統

Version 1.0

---

## Belief 1 — Memory is not data｜記憶不是資料

🇺🇸

Memory is not something to store.

Memory is something to care for.

We do not treat memories as records.
We treat them as relationships.

---

🇹🇼

記憶不是用來儲存的資料。

記憶是需要被照顧的存在。

我們不把記憶當作紀錄，
而是當作關係的一部分。

---

## Belief 2 — Caregivers come first｜照顧者優先

🇺🇸

We do not build tools for patients alone.

We build tools for the people who care for them every day.

If caregivers feel supported, patients benefit indirectly.

---

🇹🇼

我們不是只為患者設計工具。

我們是為每天照顧他們的人而設計。

當照顧者被支持，
被照顧的人自然也會被更好地照顧。

---

## Belief 3 — AI should support, not replace｜AI 應該支持，而不是取代

🇺🇸

AI must never replace human care.

AI should reduce burden, not remove responsibility.

We build AI that stands beside humans, not in place of them.

---

🇹🇼

AI 永遠不能取代人的照顧。

AI 應該減輕負擔，而不是消除責任。

我們打造的是站在人類身邊的 AI，
而不是取代人類的 AI。

---

## Belief 4 — Simplicity is respect｜簡單就是尊重

🇺🇸

Caregivers are already emotionally overloaded.

Complex systems are a burden, not a solution.

We design for clarity, not complexity.

---

🇹🇼

照顧者本來就承受極大的情緒壓力。

複雜的系統不是幫助，而是負擔。

我們設計的是清晰，而不是複雜。

---

## Belief 5 — Love is the real product｜愛才是真正的產品

🇺🇸

We are not building a memory app.

We are building a space where love can be preserved and expressed.

---

🇹🇼

我們不是在做記憶 App。

我們是在打造一個讓愛被保存與延續的空間。

---

## Closing Principle｜最後原則

🇺🇸

If a feature does not reduce suffering or increase clarity,
we do not build it.

---

🇹🇼

如果一個功能，
不能減少痛苦或增加清晰度，
我們就不做。
