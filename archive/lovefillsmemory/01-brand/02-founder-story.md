# Founder Story｜創辦人故事

Version 1.0

---

## Why I Started｜我為什麼開始

🇺🇸

I did not start Love Fills Memory as a technology project.

I started it as a personal responsibility.

I am caring for my mother, who is experiencing memory loss.

Over time, I realized something painful:

It is not only memory that fades,
but also the small moments that hold a family together.

I could not find a tool that truly understood this experience.

So I decided to build one.

---

🇹🇼

我創立 Love Fills Memory，
不是因為技術，而是因為責任。

我正在照顧逐漸失去記憶的媽媽。

在這個過程中，我發現一件很難過的事：

消失的不只是記憶，
還有那些維繫一個家庭的日常片刻。

我找不到一個真正理解這種經驗的工具。

所以，我決定自己做一個。

---

## The Moment It Became Clear｜真正看清的那一刻

🇺🇸

One day, I realized something simple but powerful:

My mother was still full of love,
even when memory became unclear.

That made me rethink everything.

Maybe the goal is not to fix memory.

Maybe the goal is to **preserve love inside memory loss**.

---

🇹🇼

有一天我突然明白：

即使記憶模糊了，
媽媽依然充滿愛。

這讓我重新思考一切。

也許目標不是「修復記憶」，

而是**在記憶流失的過程中，保留愛**。

---

## Why This Company Exists｜這家公司為什麼存在

🇺🇸

Love Fills Memory exists because caregivers need support that current tools do not provide.

We are not building to replace care.

We are building to support those who give care every day.

---

🇹🇼

Love Fills Memory 的存在，
是因為照顧者需要真正被理解的支持工具。

我們不是要取代照顧。

我們是要支持那些每天付出照顧的人。

---

## Closing Thought｜最後的話

🇺🇸

This company started with one simple moment:

Helping my mother before sleep.

Everything else is built from that.

---

🇹🇼

這家公司開始於一個很簡單的瞬間：

我第一次發現，媽媽開始慢慢忘記我們的對話。

所有的一切，
都是從這裡延伸出來的。
