## 🇺🇸 Mission

We help caregivers capture daily care moments and turn them into structured, meaningful memory with emotional support.

We reduce emotional burden through simple and thoughtful AI.

我們幫助照顧者，
把日常照護的片刻轉化為有結構、有意義的記錄，
並提供情感支持。

我們透過簡單且有溫度的 AI，
減輕照顧者的情緒負擔。
