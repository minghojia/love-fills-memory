# Vision｜願景

Version 1.0

---

## Our Vision｜我們的願景

🇺🇸

We envision a world where no caregiver feels alone.

Where technology quietly supports families with compassion.

Where memories are preserved with dignity.

Where love continues to connect generations, even when memory fades.

---

🇹🇼

我們希望建立一個世界：

沒有照顧者需要獨自承擔。

科技在背後默默支持每一個家庭。

每一段人生都能有尊嚴地被記錄。

即使記憶漸漸流失，

愛，依然能連結每一個世代。

---

## Our Long-Term Dream｜我們的長期夢想

🇺🇸

One day, Love Fills Memory will become the most trusted companion for families living with dementia.

Not because we have the most advanced AI,

but because families know we genuinely understand their journey.

---

🇹🇼

有一天，

Love Fills Memory 將成為失智家庭最值得信賴的陪伴者。

不是因為我們擁有最先進的 AI，

而是因為每一個家庭都知道，

我們真正理解他們走過的路。

---

## Beyond Today｜超越今天

🇺🇸

We believe the future of care is not replacing people with technology.

It is helping people love each other better.

---

🇹🇼

我們相信，

照護的未來，

不是用科技取代人。

而是幫助人，

更好地去愛彼此。
