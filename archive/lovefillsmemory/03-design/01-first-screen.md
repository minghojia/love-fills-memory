# First Screen Design｜首屏設計

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸

This is the first screen caregivers see after login.

It must reduce stress, not increase it.

It must feel like:

> “I finally have a place to put this.”

---

🇹🇼

這是照顧者登入後看到的第一個畫面。

它的目的不是增加功能，

而是減少壓力。

讓使用者感覺：

> 「我終於有地方可以放下這些事情。」

---

# 🧭 Design Principle｜設計原則

🇺🇸

One screen = one emotional purpose:

> Capture today’s care moment.

---

🇹🇼

一個畫面只承擔一個情緒目的：

> 記錄今天的照護瞬間

---

# 🏠 Layout Structure｜畫面結構

## 1. Top Section｜頂部

### Greeting｜問候

🇺🇸
> “How is your loved one today?”

🇹🇼
> 「今天，你的家人還好嗎？」

---

## 2. Main Input Area｜核心輸入區

### Minimal Input Box

Three options:

- ✍️ Type
- 🎙️ Speak
- 📷 Photo

No labels overload. No instructions.

Just one box.

---

### Example placeholder:

🇺🇸
> “Write what happened today…”

🇹🇼
> 「記錄今天發生的事情…」

---

## 3. Recent Memory｜最近紀錄

### Simple Timeline Preview

🇺🇸

- Today: “Refused lunch again”
- Yesterday: “Seemed more confused in morning”
- 2 days ago: “Took medication late”

---

🇹🇼

- 今天：「今天不吃午餐」
- 昨天：「早上比較混亂」
- 前天：「吃藥延遲」

---

## 4. AI Feedback Area｜AI 回饋區

After entry is submitted:

### AI responds in 3 layers:

1. Summary
2. Pattern insight (if any)
3. Emotional support

---

### Example:

🇺🇸

> “I’ve recorded this moment.
>
> This may be part of a recurring pattern with meals.
>
> You’re doing your best. I’m here with you.”

---

🇹🇼

> 「我已記錄這個情況。
>
> 這可能與近期的飲食模式有關。
>
> 你已經做得很好，我會陪著你。」

# 🚫 What This Screen MUST NOT Have

🇺🇸

- No dashboards
- No analytics
- No settings overload
- No social feed
- No ads
- No complex navigation

---

🇹🇼

- 不做儀表板
- 不做數據分析頁
- 不做複雜設定
- 不做社群動態
- 不做廣告
- 不做多層導航

---

# 🧠 Design Philosophy｜設計哲學

🇺🇸

We are not designing an app.

We are designing a **moment of relief**.

---

🇹🇼

我們不是在設計一個 App，

我們是在設計一個「喘息的瞬間」。

---

# 💡 Key Insight

The input box is not a feature.

It is:

> a safe place to put emotional weight

---

# 🌱 Success Definition

🇺🇸

If a caregiver opens this screen and immediately knows:

> “I can write something here without thinking too much.”

We succeeded.

---

🇹🇼

如果照顧者一打開畫面就知道：

> 「我可以很自然地寫下今天發生的事。」

我們就成功了。
