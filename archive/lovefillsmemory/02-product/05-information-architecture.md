🇹🇼

# 🏠 App Structure｜應用架構

## 1. Home (Timeline)｜首頁（時間軸）

### Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸
Show all care entries in chronological order.

This is the main memory stream.

🇹🇼
按時間顯示所有照護紀錄。

這是主要的記憶流。

---

### Content｜內容

- Daily entries
- Text notes
- Voice summaries
- Photos
- AI summaries

---

### Behavior｜行為

- Scroll only
- No complex filters
- No dashboards

---

## 2. Add Entry｜新增記錄

### Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸
Capture a care moment in the simplest way possible.

🇹🇼
用最簡單的方式記錄照護瞬間。

---

### Input Types｜輸入方式

- Text
- Voice
- Photo

---

### Behavior｜行為

- One tap entry
- No required fields
- Immediate AI processing after submit

---

## 3. Entry Detail｜記錄詳情

### Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸
Show AI-enhanced understanding of one care moment.

🇹🇼
呈現 AI 理解後的單一照護事件。

---

### Content｜內容

- Original input
- AI summary
- Key insights
- Emotional reflection
- Timestamp

---

## 4. Weekly View (Future)｜週視圖（未來）

### Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸
Help caregivers see patterns over time.

🇹🇼
幫助照顧者理解長期變化。

# 🚫 What We Avoid｜刻意避免

🇺🇸

- No complex menus
- No multi-layer dashboards
- No social features
- No notification overload
- No deep settings pages

---

🇹🇼

- 不做複雜選單
- 不做多層儀表板
- 不做社群功能
- 不做過度通知
- 不做複雜設定頁

---

# 🎯 First Screen Principle｜首屏原則

🇺🇸

When the caregiver opens the app, they should immediately see:

> “What happened today?”

Not:
- dashboards
- metrics
- charts

---

🇹🇼

照顧者打開 App 時，

應該立刻看到：

> 「今天發生了什麼？」

而不是：
- 儀表板
- 數據
- 圖表

---
