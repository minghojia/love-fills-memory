# Purpose｜目的

🇺🇸

This document defines who we are designing for.

While multiple groups may benefit from Love Fills Memory, not all personas have the same priority.

Every product decision should begin by asking:

> Does this improve the life of our primary caregiver?

---

🇹🇼

本文件定義 Love Fills Memory 的目標使用者。

雖然不同族群都可能從產品中受益，
但每一種角色的重要性並不相同。

每一次產品決策，都應該先問自己：

> 「這個功能，是否真的改善了主要照顧者的生活？」

# P1 — Primary Caregiver｜主要照顧者

## Who｜是誰？

🇺🇸

Usually a daughter, son, spouse, or close family member who provides day-to-day care.

Age: 40–70

This is the primary customer of Love Fills Memory.

---

🇹🇼

通常是：

- 女兒
- 兒子
- 配偶
- 其他主要照顧家人的親屬

年齡約 40–70 歲。

這是 Love Fills Memory 最重要的服務對象。

---

## Responsibilities｜日常工作

- Daily care
- Medication management
- Medical appointments
- Meal preparation
- Emotional support
- Communication with family members

---

- 日常照護
- 用藥管理
- 陪伴就醫
- 準備餐食
- 情緒支持
- 與家人溝通

---

## Pain Points｜痛點

🇺🇸

- Emotionally exhausted
- Constantly worried about forgetting something
- Notes are scattered everywhere
- No time to organize information
- Feels isolated
- Unsure if they are doing enough

---

🇹🇼

- 身心俱疲
- 擔心漏掉重要事情
- 紀錄分散在不同地方
- 沒有時間整理資訊
- 常常感到孤單
- 懷疑自己是否做得夠好

---

## Goals｜目標

🇺🇸

- Capture important daily moments
- Organize care records effortlessly
- Receive emotional support
- Reduce mental load
- Feel more confident as a caregiver

---

🇹🇼

- 記錄每天的重要照護事件
- 輕鬆整理照護紀錄
- 獲得情感支持
- 降低心理負擔
- 更有信心地照顧家人

---

## Success Looks Like｜成功代表

🇺🇸

> I feel more organized, supported, and less overwhelmed.

---

🇹🇼

> 「我終於沒有那麼慌了，也沒有那麼孤單了。」

---

# P2 — Family Member｜家屬

## Who｜是誰？

🇺🇸

Family members who are not the primary caregiver but want to stay informed and support their loved one.

Examples:

- Brother
- Sister
- Adult children living in another city or country
- Extended family members

---

🇹🇼

不是主要照顧者，

但希望了解家人的照護狀況。

例如：

- 兄弟姊妹
- 居住外地的子女
- 海外家人
- 其他親屬

---

## Goals｜目標

- Understand what happened today
- Stay connected with the family
- Support the primary caregiver
- Share responsibilities

---

- 了解今天發生什麼事
- 與家人保持同步
- 支持主要照顧者
- 分擔照護責任

---

## MVP Status｜產品階段

🇺🇸

Not part of the MVP.

We consider this persona when planning the future, but we do not optimize Version 1 for them.

---

🇹🇼

不是 MVP 的主要對象。

未來會納入設計，

但第一版產品不以此角色為優先。

---

# P3 — Healthcare Professional｜醫療／照護專業人員

## Who｜是誰？

Examples:

- Home nurse
- Occupational therapist
- Physical therapist
- Physician
- Case manager
- Social worker

---

例如：

- 居家護理師
- 職能治療師
- 物理治療師
- 醫師
- 個案管理師
- 社工

---

## Goals｜目標

- Quickly understand the patient's history
- Review caregiver observations
- Identify long-term patterns
- Improve communication with the family

---

- 快速了解照護歷程
- 查看照顧者紀錄
- 發現長期趨勢
- 提升與家屬溝通效率

---

## MVP Status｜產品階段

Future.

No features should be designed specifically for this persona until the caregiver experience is exceptional.

---

未來規劃。

在主要照顧者體驗做到非常好之前，

不會優先設計此角色專屬功能。

---

# P4 — Person Living with Dementia｜失智者本人

## Who｜是誰？

🇺🇸

The person receiving care.

Although they may gradually lose memories, they never lose their dignity, emotions, or need for love.

---

🇹🇼

接受照護的人。

即使記憶逐漸流失，

仍然擁有尊嚴、

情感、

以及被愛的需要。

---

## Our Commitment｜我們的承諾

🇺🇸

We will never design technology that treats people as data.

Every feature should preserve dignity, respect, and human connection.

---

🇹🇼

我們永遠不會把失智者視為資料。

每一個功能，

都應該維護他們的尊嚴、

尊重、

以及與家人的連結。

---

## Product Status｜產品定位

Long-term Vision.

Our first product is designed for caregivers.

Our long-term mission is to improve life for both caregivers and the people they love.

---

長期願景。

第一版產品以照顧者為核心。

長遠而言，

我們希望同時改善照顧者與失智者的生活品質。

---

# Product Decision Rule｜產品決策原則

🇺🇸

When priorities conflict:

1. Primary Caregiver
2. Family Member
3. Healthcare Professional

The needs of the primary caregiver always come first in MVP.

---

🇹🇼

當不同角色需求發生衝突時：

1. 主要照顧者
2. 家屬
3. 醫療／照護專業人員

MVP 永遠優先滿足主要照顧者的需求。

---

# Guiding Principle｜核心理念

🇺🇸

We are preserving two stories at the same time:

- The story of the person living with memory loss.
- The story of the person who loves and cares for them.

Love Fills Memory exists to honor both.

---

🇹🇼

我們同時保存兩個故事：

- 一個是失智者的人生故事。
- 一個是照顧者一路走來的故事。

Love Fills Memory，

希望用愛，

珍藏這兩段同樣重要的人生旅程。
