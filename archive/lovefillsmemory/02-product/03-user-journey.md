# Caregiver Journey｜照顧者旅程

Version 1.0

---

# 1. Context Before Using the App｜使用前的狀態

🇺🇸

The caregiver is already mentally occupied.

They are not “starting a task.”

They are in the middle of life.

Common context:

- Thinking about meals for their loved one
- Managing medication schedules
- Worried about changes in behavior
- Emotionally tired but still responsible

They do not open the app with intention.

They open it in-between life moments.

---

🇹🇼

照顧者的狀態通常是「已經很忙」。

他們不是開始一個任務，

而是在生活的中間。

常見情境：

- 思考今天要吃什麼
- 記得吃藥時間
- 擔心行為變化
- 情緒疲憊但仍需照顧

他們不是「主動打開 App」，

而是在生活的空隙中使用。

---

# 2. Trigger Moment｜觸發時刻

🇺🇸

A small moment happens:

- The loved one refuses food
- A behavior changes slightly
- A medication is missed
- Or something feels “different”

The caregiver wants to remember it.

But they don’t have time to organize it.

So they open the app.

---

🇹🇼

一個小事件發生：

- 拒絕吃飯
- 行為變化
- 忘記吃藥
- 或「感覺不太一樣」

照顧者想記住這件事，

但沒有時間整理。

於是打開 App。

---

# 3. First Action｜第一個動作

🇺🇸

The caregiver chooses the easiest possible input:

### Options:

- Type a short sentence
- Speak a quick voice note
- Take a photo (meal, environment, situation)

Example inputs:

- Mom refused lunch again
- Voice: She seems confused today
- Photo: half-eaten meal on table

No structure required.

No categories.

No thinking required.

---

🇹🇼

照顧者選擇最簡單的輸入方式：

### 可以：

- 打一句話
- 語音快速說明
- 拍一張照片（食物／環境／狀況）

例如：

- 「媽媽今天又不吃午餐」
- 語音：「她今天有點混亂」
- 照片：半份未吃完的餐點

不需要分類

不需要格式

不需要整理

---

# 4. AI Processing｜AI 處理

🇺🇸

The system processes the input:

### AI does:

- Extract key meaning
- Identify possible pattern (if any)
- Create structured summary
- Store timeline entry

But most importantly:

It responds emotionally.

---

🇹🇼

系統會處理輸入內容：

### AI 會：

- 擷取重點
- 判斷是否有模式
- 生成結構化摘要
- 存入時間軸

但最重要的是：

AI 會給出情緒回應。

---

# 5. AI Response｜AI 回應

🇺🇸

The AI responds in a calm, supportive tone:

> “I’ve recorded today’s moment.
>
> It seems mealtime is becoming more difficult.
>
> You don’t have to handle everything alone.
>
> I’m here with you.”

---

🇹🇼

AI 會用平靜、支持的語氣回應：

> 「我已經幫你記錄今天的狀況。
>
> 看起來用餐變得比較困難。
>
> 你不需要一個人承擔所有事情。
>
> 我會陪著你。」

---

# 6. Emotional Outcome｜情緒結果

🇺🇸

After using the app, the caregiver does not feel “productive.”

They feel:

- Slightly lighter
- Less mentally overloaded
- More seen
- Less alone

The goal is not efficiency.

The goal is emotional relief.

---

🇹🇼

使用完 App 後，

照顧者不一定覺得「做完事情」，

但會感覺：

- 心情稍微輕一點
- 壓力減少
- 被理解
- 不那麼孤單

重點不是效率，

而是情緒上的緩解。

---

# Product Principle Reflected｜產品原則

🇺🇸

We design for:

> “I can breathe a little easier now.”

Not:

> “I finished organizing my data.”

---

🇹🇼

我們設計的目標是：

> 「我現在可以稍微喘一口氣了。」

而不是：

> 「我完成了資料整理。」
