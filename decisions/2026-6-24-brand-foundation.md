# Decision: Establish Brand Foundation First

Date: 2026-06-24

---

## Summary

We decided to build the Brand Bible and knowledge system before writing full production code.

---

## Context

We are starting Love Fills Memory, an AI product for dementia caregivers.

We initially focused on:
- Next.js setup
- backend structure
- AI features
- product MVP

But we realized the risk:

If we build without a clear identity,
the product will drift.

---

## Options considered

### Option 1: Start coding MVP immediately
- Pros: fast progress
- Cons: unclear direction, risk of building wrong product

### Option 2: Define brand, values, and principles first
- Pros: clear direction, long-term consistency
- Cons: slower start

---

## Decision

We chose Option 2:
Build Brand Foundation first.

---

## Reasoning

Because:

- This is not just a SaaS product
- This is a long-term caregiving brand
- Decisions must stay consistent over years
- AI will amplify whatever foundation we give it

---

## Outcome

We will now build:

- Brand Bible
- Founder Story
- Brand Belief
- Product Principles

before scaling engineering work.

---

## Revisit conditions

We revisit this decision if:

- product becomes unclear to users
- brand messaging becomes inconsistent
- users misunderstand purpose of Love Fills Memory
